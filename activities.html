The Washington State Academic RedShirts Program at the University of Washington Seattle.

As a member of UW's STARS Cohort V, I interact and study alongside peers in subjects essential for engineering and learn how to navigate college and the tech world.

Coding to me is a way to create something that others can admire and utilize in their daily life. Whether it is a student turning in a homework assignment or a child playing games with their friends, the platform they are all on stems from programming.

I began programming when I learned from the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. After two months with my female peers, I walked out of the program understanding basic topics in computer science and with a determination to delve into it further.

To learn more about coding and other tech fields, I joined my school's MESA Robotics club and took AP Computer Science.
